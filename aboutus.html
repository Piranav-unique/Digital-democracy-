Logic Behind The Name - Digital Democracy

Digital:Refers to the use of technology and online platforms to facilitate democratic processes.

Democracy: Represents the idea of citizen participation and decision-making in governance.

Enhancing Public Participation: Aims to increase citizen involvement in governance through digital platforms.

Promoting Transparency: Utilizes technology to make government actions and decisions more visible and accountable.

Improving Accessibility: Ensures that more people can engage with democratic processes regardless of location or physical ability.



Vision

Universal Access to Information:Ensure that all citizens have access to accurate and comprehensive information through digital means, fostering informed decision-making and active participation in democratic processes.

Increased Transparency and Accountability:Utilize digital tools to make government actions and decisions more transparent, holding public officials accountable and fostering trust in democratic institutions.

Inclusivity and Equity: Strive to include diverse voices and perspectives in the democratic process, ensuring that marginalized and underrepresented groups have equal opportunities to participate and be heard.

Secure and Private Digital Environment: Establish robust security and privacy measures to protect citizens' data and ensure the integrity of digital democratic processes
